History

Our Counter-Strike teams are the foundation of our success. Since the beginning of 2002, mousesports' teams have dominated the German CS leagues, collecting 18 national championships and many international titles. Throughout Esports history, mousesports stood for teamwork, integrity and ambition and our teams uphold a legacy over a decade long of Counter-Strike excellence made in Germany.
